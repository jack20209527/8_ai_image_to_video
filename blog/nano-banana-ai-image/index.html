Grok Ani Appears: How to Enable Grok's Companion Mode?

Recently, Elon Musk's artificial intelligence company xAI launched a new feature for the chatbot Grok - "companion mode". Simply put, it makes Grok no longer just a cold dialogue interface, but a virtual character that can "accompany you", with personality, expression, and even some emotions. You are not chatting with a bunch of code, but communicating with a "person". In this mode, the most popular character is a virtual companion named Ani. She is set in a Gothic Lolita style, with golden twin ponytails and delicate emotional feedback, as if she came out of a Japanese anime. When many people saw her, they thought of Amane Misa in "Death Note". Shortly after her release, Ani quickly became popular in European, American and Japanese communities, and users posted screenshots of their chats with her, as if sharing a segment of "daily life with a virtual girlfriend".
