Cookies and Web Beacons

Like other websites, PhotoToVideo AI uses "Cookies" to store information, including visitor preferences and pages visited. This data helps us tailor web content to your browser type and other details for an optimized user experience.

We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor activities on our Services and store certain data. These technologies include beacons (web beacons), tags, and scripts to collect and track information, improving and analyzing our Services.

Technology Description Purpose
Cookies or Browser Cookies Small files placed on your computer, mobile device, or other equipment containing browsing history details. You can set your browser to refuse Cookies, but some Service features may not work without them unless adjusted.
Web Beacons Small electronic files in parts of our Services and emails, known as transparent GIFs, pixel tags, or single-pixel GIFs. Allow us to count users who visited pages or opened emails and gather related website statistics.

Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies remain on your device when offline, while session Cookies are deleted when you close your browser.

We use session and persistent Cookies for:

  • Essential/Necessary Cookies (Session Cookies, managed by us): Critical for providing Services and enabling certain features, such as user authentication and fraud prevention.
  • Cookie Policy/Notice Acceptance Cookies (Persistent Cookies, managed by us): Identify if you’ve accepted Cookie use on our site.
  • Functional Cookies (Persistent Cookies, managed by us): Remember your choices, like login details or language preferences, for a personalized experience.

For more details on our Cookie usage and your choices, please refer to our Cookie Policy or the Cookie section of this Privacy Policy.
